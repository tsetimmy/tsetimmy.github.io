Brief Biography

I am a graduate student at the University of Waterloo supervised by Prof. Pascal Poupart. I am associated with the Computational Statistics Group.

My research interests broadly encompass the intersection of optimization, statistics, control and machine learning. My recent work involves investigating techniques from optimization and statistics for improving the sample efficiency of (deep) reinforcement learning algorithms.

Previously, I was an undergraduate student studying electrical and computer engineering at the University of Toronto.
